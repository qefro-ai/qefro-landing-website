## Home-Specific Rules
- **Primary CTA:** Accent cyan `#0891B2` (Start Free)
- **Secondary CTA:** Purple outline `#7C3AED` (Book Demo)
- **Hero background:** White with soft muted wash — not full `#FAF5FF` page wash
- **Alt sections:** `--color-muted` `#ECEEF9`
- **Typography:** Plus Jakarta Sans only; hero titles weight 800; section heads 700
- **Motion:** Stagger grid items on scroll (max 1–2 motion accents per viewport); respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- **No** infinite decorative pulse; no emoji icons
- Keep existing platform messaging (do not revert to chatbot positioning)
