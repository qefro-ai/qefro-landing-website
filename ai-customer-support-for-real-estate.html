AI Customer Support for Real Estate Agencies

Qefro provides AI customer support for real estate agencies: cited answers for buyers and renters, optional WhatsApp, secure business actions, and staff assistants in one AI Workspace Platform.

AI customer support for real estate agencies helps buyers and renters get fast, accurate answers about your brokerage — without hiring a 24/7 team for every repetitive question.

Typical questions include “Is this listing still available?”, “What are HOA fees?”, and “Can I schedule a viewing?”. Qefro grounds replies in your uploaded policies, crawled site pages, and handbooks, with source citations and refusal when nothing relevant exists.

When chat must do more than answer, connect your systems: capture a viewing-request lead to your crm api; share brochure and disclosure pdfs with citations; escalate negotiation questions to a human agent. Credentials stay encrypted; actions can use end-user identity via identify().

Staff get a branded Internal Portal for compliance scripts and transaction checklists, while Customer AI stays on the channels you enable. Website chat for listings; WhatsApp for touring logistics.

Public listing answers stay in public workspaces; offer docs stay private to agents. Start with a 14-day free trial, then scale workspaces as locations grow.

How Qefro delivers AI Customer Support for Real Estate Agencies

Configure knowledge, instructions, and Business Tools once in the Admin Console. Deploy Customer AI on your website widget and WhatsApp, and Employee AI on a branded Internal Portal — with the same retrieval, permissions, and action layer underneath.

Answers are grounded in your documents and crawled pages. Hybrid search combines keyword and vector retrieval, returns source citations, and is designed to decline when nothing relevant exists — so teams can trust support and internal assistants in production.
