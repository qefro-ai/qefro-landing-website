Authenticated users

Integrate signed-in customers in minutes

Qefro is not an identity provider. Your app owns login and JWTs. Forward identity to Business Tools with identify() — passwords never touch Qefro.

const widget = new Widget({
  token: "..."
});

widget.identify({
  id: user.id,
  email: user.email,
  auth: {
    mode: "jwt",
    token: userJwt
  }
});

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

Qefro is an AI customer support platform. It answers from your knowledge base, securely calls your existing business APIs (Business Tools / OpenAPI), and deploys to website and WhatsApp — without replacing your authentication system.

Qefro is freemium — start Free forever (100 conversations/month, 2 documents, 1 Business Tool). Starter is $29/month billed annually ($39 monthly, 5 Business Tools). Growth is $99/month billed annually ($119 monthly, unlimited Business Tools). Enterprise is custom. No credit card required.

PDFs, Word documents, Markdown, plain text — or crawl entire websites automatically. Our pipeline processes and indexes everything.

Qefro retrieves only from your verified content and is designed to decline answering when no relevant information exists in your knowledge base, rather than guessing. See our benchmark methodology for how we evaluate accuracy and refusal behavior.

Your data is tenant-isolated and workspace-isolated, encrypted at rest and in transit, and never used to train AI models. API secrets are encrypted; end-user passwords are never stored. Private deployment available for Enterprise. We are building toward SOC 2; contact sales for the current compliance roadmap.
