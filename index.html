Why Qefro

Confident answers — or an honest “I don’t know”

Generic chatbots fill the gaps by guessing. Qefro answers only from your verified content, shows its sources, and declines when the answer isn’t there.

A customer asks

“Can I still get a refund after 45 days?”

Generic chatbot

“Absolutely! You can request a full refund any time within 60 days of purchase.”

Invents a policy that doesn’t exist — confident, and wrong.

Qefro

“Our refund window is 30 days, so an order from 45 days ago isn’t eligible. I can connect you with the support team if you’d like.”

Answered from: Refund Policy.pdf

Grounded in your content

Every answer comes from sources you’ve approved — nothing invented, nothing off-brand.

Shows its sources

Each response links back to the document it used, so your team can verify every claim.

Declines, doesn’t guess

When your knowledge base doesn’t cover a question, it says so instead of making something up.
