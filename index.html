Security

Enterprise-ready by design

Multi-tenant architecture with workspace isolation, team-based RBAC, encrypted secrets, and conversation isolation — so one organization never sees another’s data.

Multi-tenant isolation

Tenant boundaries at the database and vector store. No cross-organization data leakage.

Workspace & conversation isolation

Knowledge, tools, and threads stay scoped to the workspaces and permissions you define.

Team-based RBAC

Owner, Admin, and Member roles control documents, Business Tools, billing, and invites.

Secure Business Tool execution

Encrypted secrets, SSRF protections, schema validation, and full execution logs.

Authenticated users

Your auth stays yours

Qefro is not an identity provider. Forward signed-in identity with identify() so Business Tools act on behalf of real users — passwords never touch Qefro.

widget.identify({
  id: user.id,
  email: user.email,
  auth: {
    mode: "jwt",
    token: userJwt
  }
});
