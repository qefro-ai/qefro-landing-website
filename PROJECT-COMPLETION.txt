USE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Add to your HTML:

Floating Card:
  <div data-floating-card>Content</div>

Parallax Layer:
  <div data-depth-layer="1">Background</div>
  <div data-depth-layer="2">Middle</div>

3D Text:
  <h2 data-text-3d>Animated Heading</h2>

Floating Element:
  <div class="float-3d">Content</div>

That's it! Effects work automatically.

TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK REFERENCE
================================================================================

ISSUE: Cards not responding to mouse
SOLUTION: Check data-floating-card attribute, open DevTools (F12)

ISSUE: Parallax not working
SOLUTION: Use data-depth-layer="N", try scrolling page

ISSUE: Text not animating
SOLUTION: Add data-text-3d to element, scroll to trigger

ISSUE: Performance slow
SOLUTION: Reduce floating elements, check browser FPS, test mobile

ISSUE: Effects disabled
SOLUTION: Check prefers-reduced-motion setting in OS

See README-3D-FEATURES.md for detailed troubleshooting
